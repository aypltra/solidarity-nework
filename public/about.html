Who are We, and Why Do We Do This?

We, Alexa Alcantara and Bea Moritcho, science high school students, simply want an end to all chaos. We are advocates for justice, and wish for a world of solidarity. Despite our age and limited finances, we do what we can with our own skills at hand to be able to extend our support for our Palestinian friends.





What our Website is all About

Our website effectively aids the extension of monetary donations to the innocent victims of war, by compiling all their respective donation links into one webpage, allowing for a more convenient transaction. This website also allows victims in need of financial support to input their own donation links, which will be added to the website.



Mission

Solidarity Network aims to serve as a catalyst for getting people from around the world together, no matter where they may be, to be able support the vulnerable and afflicted.



Vision

We wish for a future in which we are not needed
