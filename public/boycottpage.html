Here are companies and artists who have expressed support for Israel






How do we Boycott?


We can boycott by simply refusing to purchase the goods and/or services provided these companies. We may also publicly express our disapproval towards these companies and artists in order for others to know about this situation



Why We Must Boycott


This is a very simple way to help out the countless victims of this war. Doing this lessens the funding of the destructive operations of Israel against Palestine. By simply choosing not to support these companies and artists, we become a part of a cause, in which, with collective efforts, can save multiple lives.
