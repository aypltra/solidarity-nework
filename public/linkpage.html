OTHER LINKS

Here are some authorized organizations whom you may donate to

United Nation's Crises Reliefer

This organization supports urgent relief for the victims of the Palestinian Crisis. Your donations toward this cause will help aid the highest priority humanitarian activities managed by the United Office For Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs.

Donate to this organization

United Nation's Population Fund

This organization works to provide support to the most vulnerable victims of conflict. UNFPA specifically puts their focus on pregnant women and newborns, and with your donation, you can be able to provide well-needed life-saving services to new and expecting mothers.

Donate to this organization

Palestine Red Cresent Society

This is an officially recognized independent Palestinian National Society part of Red Cross and Red Cresent Movement whose mission is to provide humanitarian, social, and health services to Palestinians. Proceeds go to a variety of programs that help the victims of conflict.

Donate to this organization

American Near East Refugee Aid

This organization (since 1968) has been helping thousands refugees and victims from Palestine, Jordan and Lebanon. They mobilize resources need for emergency relief and long term development.

Donate to this organization

Middle Eastern Children's Alliance

MECA is a non profit organization that focuses on providing aid for children and their families in Palestine and Syria. They advocate for the rights and well-being of the children belonging in the Middle East.

Donate to this organization

Save The Children

Save The Children, as the name suggests, help the victimized children of the Israeli-Palestine conflict. They are currently in West Bank and Gaza, and strive to meet the immediate needs of the children and their families in that area.

Donate to this organization

Palestine Children's Relief Fund

PRCS is another organization that aims to extend support to the children currently residing in Palestine. They provide critical medical care to Palestinian victims, and especially aim to support the vulnerable children in the area.

Donate to this organization
